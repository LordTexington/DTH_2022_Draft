ABOUT US

Australian-based DTH Drilling Solutions was founded
by Graeme and Sam Exelby in 2006.

The Company is an independent provider of solutions-
focused coaching for production drilling in hard rock mines.

Graeme has a career-long background in surface and
underground drilling industry. In addition, he had 10 years involvement in high level product development and failure analysis.

Our programs target efficiency and the principles of drilling and as such are not machine specific.
