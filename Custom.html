CUSTOMISED PROGRAMS

Our programs comprise two sections being Level 1 Audit of drillers, drills and drilling and Level 2 Driller Excellence Package both of which are customised to your operation. Level 1 includes observations and evaluations of drillers, supervisors and trainers to benchmark current performance and develop a gap analysis. Level 2 consists of comprehensive theory covering major drill components, compressed air fundamentals, drilling principles, efficiency and safety. The practical sessions involve one-on-one time on the machine to put theory into practice and is followed by a written assessment.

LEVEL 1 LEVEL 2
  • Initial audit on the job to benchmark drillers
  • Evaluation of Skills, Knowledge and Disposition (SKD)
  • Develop GAP analysis and provide initial coaching
  • Theory session covering principles of drilling, efficiency, etc.
  • One-on-one coaching to put theory into practice
  • Evaluation of Skills, Knowledge and Disposition (SKD)
  • Certificates of Competency after written assessment
